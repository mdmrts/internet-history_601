INVENTION OF THE INTERNET

Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA), later renamed the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) the agency was created on February 7, 1958 by President Dwight D. Eisenhower in response to the Soviet launching of Sputnik 1 in 1957.

DOT COM BUBBLE

The dotcom bubble is a stock market bubble that was caused by speculation in dotcom or internet-based businesses from 1995 to 2000. The companies were largely those with a “.com” domain on their internet address.

ABOUT THE INTERNET

The Domain Name System (DNS) is the phonebook of the Internet. Humans access information online through domain names, like nytimes.com or espn.com. Web browsers interact through Internet Protocol (IP) addresses.
